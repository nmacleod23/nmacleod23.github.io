Final Project

My program is a sudoku puzzle. Click on a difficulty button to start the game. The goal is to have one of each number in each row, column, and 3x3 square. To do so, click on one of the number buttons, then click on a tile. Tiles with red numbers on them cannot be changed. Click the "Check Answer" button to see if what you put down is right or not. On easy mode, this is done automatically. When all the squares are correct, the win screen pops up.
